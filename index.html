About Us

SHINING SERVICES INDIA PVT LTD is a reliable team to execute any design to serve dreams to clients. The designs are framed around clients and not the other way around. SHINING SERVICES INDIA PVT LTD is a developing brand with a multidisciplinary creative group.

We have contributed to the development of many auditoriums, residential, and commercial projects. We have emerged as a full-fledged interior design service provider, executing projects directly under leading architects and clients.

Our forte lies in residences, corporate offices, banks, malls, hotels, restaurants, and media houses. "Innovative Interiors is the definition of refined perfection!" Our aim is to bring to our readers a steady set of resources that would help them visualize, create, and maintain beautiful homes.
